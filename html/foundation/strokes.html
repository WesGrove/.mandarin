2. Stroke-Order Principles

  1. Horizontal before vertical
  2. Piě (丿) before nà (㇏)
  3. Top to bottom
  4. Left to right
  5. Outside before inside
  6. Close the frame last
  7. Centre before sides
  8. Uppermost dot first
