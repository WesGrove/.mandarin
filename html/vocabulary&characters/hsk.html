5. Logistics & Tips

  • Skills tested: Listening, Reading, Writing
  • Speaking: separate HSKK test (often bundled with L3-6)
  • Medium: Simplified Chinese only
  • Bookings: 500+ centres worldwide via chinesetest.cn
  • Fees: USD 30 – 90 (level/location)
⚠️ If a school asks for “HSK 5”, confirm which version they recognise before booking.
